Providers of service

All of the data is stored in a postgresql database provided by heroku.com, which the entire service is hosted on. It uses the TLS 1.3 HTTPS encryption to make sure the data submitted is being transported securely.

Data collection and related actions

Further, just-chat doesn't collect any data other that the ones integral to the working of the service (the only data analyzed is registration information [username, password, date of creation], sent messages and the 'Contant Us' form data). We do not collect any data about the user's location/ hardware and software specification, etc. All of the data related to your account can be downloaded here, deleted here, for any feedback and inquiries contact us here.

Cookies

just-chat uses cookies to keep you logged in and also to maintain a socket connection and also to keep you logged in upon reconnect.

In case of a data breach

In case a data breach is detected, all of the services will be taken down for maintenance and all of the service interfaces will display notification disclosing all known information about the breach (date of occurence, possible data leaked, etc.).
